Lyrical Book Cover

Challenge: In my graphic design class, I was tasked to design a book cover based off of a song of my choice. The goal was to capture the spirit of the song, and not just the literal meaning of the song title.

Solution: I captured the ambiguous, lonely feeling of the song by arranging a symmetrical, contrasting layout; but with a red crow as a twist.
