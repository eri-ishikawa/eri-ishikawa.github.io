Overview

My Role: Developed user experience and designed wireframes of interfaces, with supervision from Lead Designer

Problem: Weekly meetings are arranged manually every semester by comparing 30+ team members' schedules. This results in hours of wasted time for the company.

Goals: Reusing as many existing assets as reasonable, design an interface for the internal website focused on quickly identifying the best times when team members are available for weekly meetings.
